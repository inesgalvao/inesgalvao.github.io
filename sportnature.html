DECEMBER 2022

SPORT NATURE

Sport Nature was a project developed in the junior enterprise jeKnowledge. The application aims to use sport as a means of education and promotion of healthy living habits. Throughout the practice of sports in nature it is intended to encourage users to improve their levels of physical activity and to have a better cultural and environmental knowledge of the municipality of Vila Nova de Poiares. It also promotes the culture and knowledge associated with the countries that form its consortium. My work was restricted to the creation of the user interface and its respective visual identity.
