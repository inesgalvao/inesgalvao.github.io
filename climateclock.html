MARCH 2021

CLIMATE CLOCK COIMBRA

Climate Clock Coimbra was a project developed by the junior enterprises jeKnowledge and molecular.je . The Climate Clock emerged from a collaboration between scientists, artists and activists. The clock serves as a tool to pressure government decision-makers and the general population to take action for the planet. My work focused on the development of a visual identity, a website and its communication elements (social media posts, documentation for partners, etc.).
