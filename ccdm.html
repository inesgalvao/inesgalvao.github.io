JUNE 2022

CICLO DE CONVERSAS -
DESIGN + MULTIMÉDIA

Project developed individually, in a context of experimentation, based on the statement of the subject Project 1. The work aimed to create a visual identity for the event "Ciclo de Conversas - Design + Multimédia". This cycle of conferences takes place in the scope of the Degree and Master in Design and Multimedia of the University of Coimbra, gathering different renowned professionals in the area of Design.
